MarineBio is deeply committed to marine conservation and founded on the concept that,
by sharing the wonders of the ocean and marine life, people will be inspired to protect it.
We hope you will consider becoming a MarineBio Conservation Society member to help us bring
the ocean and the conservation message to as many people as possible. There are many other
organizations working on marine conservation and other environmental issues such as
biodiversity and global warming. We list them here both as a public service and to spread
the word. Please Contact us if you have any comments, changes, or suggestions. For an
objective annotated list of the organizations listed below and others, visit Mother Jones
magazine's guide to environmental organizationsexternal link.
